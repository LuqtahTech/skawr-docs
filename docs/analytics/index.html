Skawr Analytics Pre-build

Smart MENA-first product analytics that tells you what's happening — without you asking — and groups your users by how they actually behave.

What it is

A new product line for Skawr — separate from the marketplace business. Same category as Amplitude / Mixpanel / PostHog: drop in an SDK, events flow up, you get funnels, retention, user journeys, drop-offs. We compete on positioning, not feature count.

Who it's for: B2B product / growth teams running web or mobile apps. Two priority segments:

  1. MENA-based product teams (Saudi, UAE, Egypt) where Amplitude / Mixpanel feel foreign — wrong currency, no Arabic, no local payment, data outside region.
  2. Global SMB startups burned by Amplitude's MTU pricing — punished for retaining users.

The four pillars

1
Proactive smart insights
System auto-detects anomalies, drivers, and hidden drop-offs. Push, not pull. No chatbot. Every insight links to the math.
2
Embeddings-powered clustering
Groups users by semantic similarity of their journey (not rule-based cohorts). Surfaces cohorts you didn't know existed. Reuses our Fireworks embeddings stack.
3
MENA-first
Arabic UI, RTL-first, Hijri calendar, Ramadan-aware comparisons, AWS me-south-1 residency, SAR/AED + Mada/Tabby/Tamara, PDPL compliance.
4
Vertical templates
Opinionated funnels, dashboards, and benchmarks per app type. MVP target: marketplaces. Phase 2: fintech, edtech.

MVP focus Marketplace-first

Strategy anchor: Saudi / UAE marketplace startups (10k–500k MAU). Pre-defined marketplace funnels (listing → contact → message → close), regional benchmarks seeded from skawr-scraper data, Arabic UI, and pricing that doesn't punish retention.

The pricing wedge

Amplitude charges by Monthly Tracked Users (MTU) — every retained user raises the bill. This is the single most-complained-about thing in the category. Our pricing flip: flat-rate or event-volume tiers. Page headline: "Never get punished for growing."
