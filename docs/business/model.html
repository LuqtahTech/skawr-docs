Business Model

Comprehensive overview of Skawr's revenue streams, cost structure, and financial projections for sustainable growth in the Saudi e-commerce ecosystem.

Business Model Overview

Skawr operates as a marketplace aggregator platform with multiple revenue streams, combining affiliate commerce, SaaS services, and advertising to create a sustainable and scalable business model tailored for the Saudi e-commerce ecosystem.

Core Value Creation

🛒
For Consumers
Free basic service with time savings, better prices, and comprehensive product discovery
🏪
For Marketplaces
Increased qualified traffic, market intelligence, and commission-based partnerships
📢
For Advertisers
Targeted reach with intent-based advertising and cross-platform presence

Strategic Summary

This business model creates multiple value creation mechanisms while building sustainable competitive advantages through network effects, data accumulation, and market positioning that become increasingly difficult for competitors to replicate over time.

Key Success Drivers

  • Network Effects: Growing user and marketplace ecosystem creates increasing value
  • Data Advantages: Search patterns and market intelligence become competitive moats
  • Revenue Diversification: Multiple streams reduce dependency risk
  • Scalable Technology: Infrastructure costs grow slower than user base
  • Market Leadership: First-mover advantage reduces acquisition costs over time
