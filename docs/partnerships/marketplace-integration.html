  • Product catalog size and structure
  • Update frequency requirements
  • Preferred contact method for coordination
2

Technical Integration

Duration: 3-5 business days

Our Process:

  • Configure our scraper service for your platform
  • Set up data processing pipeline
  • Implement product categorization
  • Configure search optimization

  • Search result validation
  • Link integrity testing
  • Performance optimization

Go Live & Monitoring

  • Dedicated partnership support

📋 Integration Requirements

🌐 Technical Requirements

✅ Public website with accessible product pages
✅ Consistent URL structure for product listings
✅ Product images hosted on reliable CDN
⭐ API endpoints (preferred but not required)

📊 Content Requirements

✅ Product titles, descriptions, and prices
✅ High-quality product images
✅ Seller/vendor information
⭐ Product categories and specifications

🤝 Business Requirements

✅ Valid business license in Saudi Arabia
✅ Commitment to maintain data accuracy
✅ Compliance with local e-commerce regulations
⭐ Dedicated partnership contact

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does integration typically take?
Most integrations are completed within 7-10 business days from initial contact to going live.
Do you charge for integration?
Our basic integration is completely free. Premium partnerships operate on a revenue-sharing model that benefits both parties.
How often is product data updated?
Data is updated every 4-6 hours for most partners, with hourly updates available for premium partnerships.
Can we control which products are included?
Yes, we can work with you to configure filtering rules and category selections based on your preferences.
